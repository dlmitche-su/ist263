Past | Present Day | Future

Future drone use for people and the government alike is more of a grayscale area; no one ever knows how they can evolve, but we know that we are not done pushing the limits of what can be done to them and making them even more advanced and aware as time goes on. We are evolving further to even get drones to land on the largest moon, Saturn, to examine the various types of soil on the planet and examine the area that was only once been able to be seen by the moon landers. Going on, drones are considered to be used for taxi and emergency services that could benefit and help the masses in aid and reduce the time consumption of destination routes by high percentages. Security drones are also another future-driven idea with the help of Artificial Intelligence, they can benefit from not having any person injured and reduce the risk of liabilities, and in addition, they provide 24/7 live feed without any hang-ups.
